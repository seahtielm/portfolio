My Dream Destinations

Exploring the world through imagination: A curated collection of places I hope to visit someday.

New York, Times Square

The City That Never Sleeps

Even though I've never actually been there, New York City is totally my dream. It's that place in all the movies and songs, the city that never sleeps, where anything can happen. I picture myself walking down those busy streets, the yellow cabs whizzing by, and all those crazy bright lights of Times Square.

Best time to visit: September to November
Famous for: Broadway, Times Square, Central Park

I want to get lost in the art museums, catch a real Broadway show, and eat pizza from a tiny hole-in-the-wall place. It's like the whole city is just bursting with stories, and I want to be a part of it, even if just for a little while. I can't wait to finally experience it, wear the outfit I always dream of, and pretend like I'm a runway model visiting New York.

Shibuya, Japan

Urban Energy and Culture

Shibuya feels special to me, even though I haven't been there yet. I imagine it's a place that perfectly captures the vibrant, electric energy of the city—just like the bustling streets, the neon lights, and the constant flow of people.

Best time to visit: Spring (March-May) or Autumn (September-November)
Famous for: Shibuya Crossing, Fashion, Street Culture

It sounds like the kind of place that makes you feel alive, where there's always something happening. As a city girl at heart, I feel like Shibuya represents everything I love about urban life—the style, the culture, the creativity. The thought of being surrounded by so much energy, fashion, and innovation just draws me in.

Paris, France

City of Love and Magic

Paris is more than a destination—it's a feeling, a whisper of romance and art. It's the golden light on the Seine, the scent of fresh bread, and the echo of church bells. It's dressing up for the joy of it, wandering without a plan, and finding beauty in hidden courtyards.

Best time to visit: April to mid-June, September to November
Famous for: Eiffel Tower, Louvre, Champs-Élysées

It's not just a city, it's a feeling of magic, a place where anything seems possible. The food is a dream—fresh croissants, gooey cheese, and those little bistros with red-checkered tablecloths. More than anything, it's that timeless beauty that just makes you feel like anything is possible.

Santorini, Greece

Island of Beauty and Tranquility

I have always been fascinated by the Greek islands, and I have heard that Santorini is one of the most beautiful places in the world. The whitewashed buildings, blue-domed churches, and stunning views of the Aegean Sea have captured my imagination.

Best time to visit: May to June, September to October
Famous for: White Buildings, Sunset Views, Volcanic Landscape

I would love to relax on the unique beaches, explore the charming villages, and immerse myself in the local culture. The prospect of enjoying fresh Greek food and wine while overlooking the stunning blue waters is a dream I can't wait to make real.

Amsterdam, Netherlands

A City of History and Culture

I have always been interested in Dutch history and culture, and Amsterdam is a great place to experience it. The city's unique blend of historical significance and modern vibrancy calls to me as a place of deep exploration and personal discovery.

Best time to visit: April to May, September to November
Famous for: Canals, Rijksmuseum, Anne Frank House

I would love to visit the Anne Frank House to connect with an important historical narrative, explore the Rijksmuseum's incredible art collections, and wander through the charming Jordaan district. Taking a canal cruise and exploring the city by bike sounds like the perfect way to experience Amsterdam's unique charm.
