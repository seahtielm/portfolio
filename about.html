About Me

Get to know who I am, my journey, and what drives my passion and creativity.

Who Am I?

Hello! I am Seahtiel Maybuena, a graduating IT student from University of the East. A passionate Data Analyst with a strong interest in Data Analysis. I have 2 years experience in Customer Service. I am highly motivated, results-oriented, and committed to delivering the best solutions. I believe in continuous learning and thrive in collaborative environments.

The Quiet Scream: My Journey

A raw and honest exploration of my life's chapters, from childhood through personal challenges

Early Childhood

Roots and Family Dynamics

Born in Dinagat Island, Surigao Del Norte, into a prominent family. Parents separated when I was 3, leaving me with my dad and stepbrothers. My family's social status protected me from bullying, with my grandfather being a governor and my father a General Director.

Parental Separation

A Complex Childhood

My mom left for Singapore, initially told I was abandoned. Later understood she left due to my father's treatment. Dad had multiple relationships, with stepmoms who were kind, but he remained the core problem. Moved between Dinagat, Davao, and Surigao City.

Growing Up

Independence and Academic Pressure

Raised by grandparents and various relatives. Developed extreme independence, doing schoolwork alone. Aunts and uncles set high academic expectations, which I consistently met by topping honor lists and receiving numerous awards.

Family Reconnection

A New Chapter

Mom returned, and I didn't recognize her initially. Moved to Manila/Singapore for vacations. Met my stepdad, initially reluctant but grew to appreciate his efforts. Chose him over my biological father, who I haven't seen in 16 years.

Teenage Years

Self-Discovery and Challenges

High school was an emotional rollercoaster. Balanced academic excellence with personal growth. Joined debate team, student council, and school newspaper. Discovered passions for writing, acting, and public speaking.

Personal Growth

Navigating Relationships

Experienced friendship challenges - betrayal, misunderstandings, and loss. Also formed lifelong friendships. Explored first crushes and the complexities of teenage emotions while maintaining focus on academic goals.

College Struggles

Unfulfilled Dreams

Heartbroken not by love, but by studying a course chosen by parents instead of my dream path. Experienced the pain of pursuing a study that wasn't my passion. Pulled all-nighters, struggled with subjects, yet persevered.

Professional Exploration

Work and Sacrifice

Worked in BPO industry for over a year. Got promoted but refused due to increased responsibilities. Chose to focus on studies and help my mom with her business. Learned that life doesn't always give you what you want.

Present

Resilience and Hope

A graduating IT student at University of the East. Continuing to grow, support my family, and pursue personal development. Grateful for my mother's hard work and unwavering support.
